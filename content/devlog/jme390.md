We are excited to announce that version 3.9.0-stable of the JMonkeyEngine game engine was released earlier this year in January of 2026.

This new version addresses a wide range of issues and includes many enhancements to the engine's rendering, audio, utilities, and much more.

The full release notes are available at https://github.com/jMonkeyEngine/jmonkeyengine/releases/tag/v3.9.0-stable

Pre-compiled libraries are available from the Maven Central repository under the "org.jmonkeyengine" groupID.


Work has also begun on the next 3.10 release, with this next version expected to bring improvements to jMEs lighting and special effects. Alpha releasess are already available for those who wish to help with testing the next 3.10 release. We've also begun planning for a future jME 4.0 release that is expected to add Vulkan support to our engine, however these plans are still very early, and our primary focus is on the upcoming 3.10 release. 

For the latest news and discussion of JMonkeyEngine software development, visit [the Development category at the JME Community Hub/Forum](https://hub.jmonkeyengine.org/c/development-discussion-jme3/9).
